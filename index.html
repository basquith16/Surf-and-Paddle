ASQUITH SURFS WAY TO VICTORY, ON FETAL SHARKS!

Anne Marie       on Monday Oct. 7th 2013 9:22pm

What could have been a huge disaster at the US National Surfing Championships in Jacksonville, turned into a heroic story of courage and devastating looks. Asquith, who was first to surf in the finals, was paddling out and getting set up for his run. As he looked back to watch the swells, a dorsal fin suddenly shot up out of the water. The dorsal fin belonged to an 18 foot Great White shark!

Asquith immediately dove off of his board as the monsterous beast snapped it in half! He then swam into the shark's mouth and was missing for several minutes. It was then that Asquith emerged with two of the shark's babies strapped to his feet! A dirty hippie from the crowd yelled, "Whoa that's radical!" 

  WOAH, THAT'S RADICAL!

All of the nearby females in attendance suddenly became wet as Asquith's wake splashed them from head to toe. He gave a big thumbs up to the crowd followed by a shrug and an, "Ayyyyyy!" When asked about the incident, Asquith was very coy and offered only the following cryptic message: Fabulous secret powers were revealed to me the day I held aloft my magic board and said, "By the powers of Mint Skoal, I have the power! If you take the good, you take the bad, you take them both and then you have, the facts of life. Standing tall, on the wings of my dream. Rise and fall, on the wings of my dream. The rain and thunder, the wind and haze, I'm bound for better days.

Those are strong words, from a strong man. Asquith will be Orlando's one and only Beefy King on November the 8th, at 7pm. Autographs will be first come, first serve. From there, Asquith will leave for the shores of Australia to train for his next adventure.

ANNE MARIE
