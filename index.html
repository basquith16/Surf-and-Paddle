ASQUITH SQUEEKS BY SWANSON, SURFING ON FETAL SHARKS!

  Anne Marie on Monday Oct 7th, 2013 9:22pm
           What could have been a huge disaster at the US National Surfing Championships in
           Jacksonville, turned into a heroic story of courage and devastating looks. Asquith,
           who was first to surf in the finals, was paddling out and getting set up for his run. As
           he looked back to watch the swells, a dorsal fin suddenly shot up out of the water.
           The dorsal fin belonged to an 18 foot Great White shark!

           Asquith immediately dove off of his board as the monsterous beast snapped it in half!
           He then swam into the shark's mouth and was missing for several minutes. It was then that
           Asquith emerged with two of the shark's babies strapped to his feet! A dirty hippie from the
           crowd yelled, "Whoa that's radical!"
         
     WOAH ,THAT'S RADICAL!
